Courses We Offer

Our campus offers a variety of courses spanning from foundational intermediate programs to specialized degree courses in fields like Engineering, Medicine, Business, Arts, and Humanities. Our postgraduate offerings include Master's degrees, PhDs, and professional certifications, allowing students to further specialize and engage in advanced studies or research. With a focus on academic excellence and practical skills development, our courses prepare students for success in their chosen careers.

Intermediate

At our campus, we offer a diverse range of educational opportunities, catering to students at various stages of their academic journey. For those fresh out of high school, our intermediate courses serve as the gateway to higher education. These programs span a variety of subjects, including Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Languages. Over the course of two years, students delve into foundational concepts, laying the groundwork for their future academic pursuits. Our intermediate courses are designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge needed to thrive in more advanced studies.

Degree

Moving on to our degree courses, students who have successfully completed their intermediate education can specialize in a field of their choice. Whether it's Engineering, Medicine, Business, Arts, or Humanities, our degree programs offer in-depth learning experiences tailored to each discipline. Through a combination of classroom instruction, practical exercises, and hands-on projects, students develop a comprehensive understanding of their chosen subject matter. Upon graduation, they earn a bachelor's degree, equipped with the expertise necessary to excel in their respective fields

Post-Graduate

For those seeking to take their education to the next level, our postgraduate courses provide a pathway to advanced studies and specialization. These programs are designed for students who have completed their bachelor's degree and wish to further refine their skills or pursue research opportunities. Whether it's a Master's degree, PhD, or professional certification, our postgraduate offerings provide a platform for intellectual growth and academic achievement. With access to world-class faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant learning community, students embark on a journey of discovery, pushing the boundaries of knowledge in their chosen field.

Where We're Located

Our global campuses in Nyeri, Nairobi and Nakuru offer an immersive educational experience, blending academic excellence with cultural diversity. With modern facilities and dedicated faculty, students embark on a journey of personal and intellectual growth. From intermediate to postgraduate courses, our campuses provide a platform for students to thrive, fostering global citizenship and community engagement.

Our Facilities

Our campus boasts top-notch facilities to enrich student life. The library offers a wealth of resources for academic exploration. Our spacious playing ground promotes physical well-being and sportsmanship. And our clean dining hall serves up tasty, nutritious meals to fuel student success.

Our Library

well-equipped library is a hub of knowledge, offering a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support academic research and learning. With comfortable study spaces and access to expert librarians, students have the perfect environment to enhance their understanding and explore new ideas.

Playing Ground

Our expansive playing ground provides students with ample opportunities for physical activity and recreation. From sports fields to outdoor courts, students can engage in various sports and leisure activities, promoting holistic development and a healthy lifestyle.

Tasty and Healthy Food

Our clean dining facilities serve tasty and nutritious meals, catering to diverse dietary preferences and ensuring students' well-being. With a focus on quality ingredients and hygienic preparation, students can enjoy delicious food in a safe and welcoming environment, fueling their bodies and minds for success.
